About Us

CrowdStrike is a leading cybersecurity company specializing in endpoint protection, threat intelligence, and cyberattack response services. As a BIT major, your skills are highly sought after in the cybersecurity sector, and CrowdStrike offers a range of opportunities for you to apply your knowledge and expertise.

BIT Major

How BIT major can fit in this role:

  • BIT majors are well-equipped to excel in roles at CrowdStrike due to their strong foundation in technology, business, and analytics. Key areas where BIT majors' skills align with CrowdStrike's needs include:

  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals: BIT majors have a solid understanding of cybersecurity principles, including network security, data protection, and risk management, which are essential for roles in threat detection and incident response at CrowdStrike.

  • Data Analysis: CrowdStrike relies on data-driven insights to identify and mitigate cyber threats. BIT majors' expertise in data analysis, including data mining, visualization, and interpretation, makes them valuable assets in analyzing security data and detecting anomalies.

  • Business Acumen: Understanding the business impact of cybersecurity threats is crucial for effective risk management. BIT majors' knowledge of business processes, financial analysis, and strategic planning enables them to assess the implications of cyber threats and develop proactive security measures.

  • Technology Proficiency: BIT majors are proficient in various technologies, including programming languages, database management systems, and network infrastructure. This technical expertise allows them to navigate CrowdStrike's advanced cybersecurity tools and platforms effectively.
